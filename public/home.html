Account Summary

The summary box contains the specific terms and conditions for this account and where applicable, these supersede our General Savings Conditions.

1. You can deposit any amount between £1 and £200

2. You can add to your savings at any time up to the £200 maximum

3. You can withdraw your money without giving us notice

4. If your balance falls below £50 your interest rate will reduce to 0.10% AER

5. All payments to and from your MyBank account must be from a personal current account held in your name with another UK bank/building society
